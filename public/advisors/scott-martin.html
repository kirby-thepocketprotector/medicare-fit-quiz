Meet Scott

Scott Martin is a Senior Medicare Advisor at The Pocket Protector with deep expertise in Medicare Advantage, Medigap, and Part D prescription drug plans. Since entering the Medicare industry, Scott has assisted over 4,000 seniors and consistently ranked among the top-performing agents in the nation — earning four President's Club awards.

Before his career in Medicare, Scott was an Electrical Systems Engineer for a nuclear plant in Illinois, where he developed the analytical precision and problem-solving discipline that now defines his approach to benefits advising. He holds a Bachelor of Science in Electrical Engineering from the Illinois Institute of Technology.

Scott's engineering background gives him a unique edge: he treats every client's Medicare decision as a systems problem — mapping doctor networks, drug formularies, cost structures, and benefit gaps to arrive at the optimal plan. His clients consistently retain their coverage at rates far above industry averages because the recommendations are built on data, not sales pressure.

When Scott is not assisting seniors in their golden years, he's an avid fisherman and will happily discuss his most recent outing any chance he gets.
